What?

This is a custom Google search engine limited to websites that apply Bayesian rationality to the subjects they discuss.

What do you mean "rationality"?

Bayesian rationality is about improving our thinking and decision-making using the best evidence available.

Rationalists are especially concerned with probability around evidence and outcomes. A lot of effort has gone into improving our decision making processes and a lot of digital ink has been spilled in this pursuit.

Why have a search engine for these sites?

These are the sites that rationalists have identified as high-quality. That means the subjects are well thought-out and the evidence provided is frequently strong or else is clearly marked when not strong.

Are other sites worthless?

We aim to make this a search engine with better than average odds of finding rationalist writings on any given subject.

Whatever subject you study, you'll probably find yourself back on Google trying to find the truth at some point. But a good first step is to check what rationalists have already found. Your second step could be anything.
